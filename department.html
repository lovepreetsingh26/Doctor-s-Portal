Arnold Luterman Regional Burn Center

Arnold Luterman Regional Burn Center is the only facility in the region providing burn care to patients from the time of their injury to recovery. The burn team is comprised of physicians specializing in burn surgery, infectious disease, plastic surgery, trauma surgery, orthopaedic surgery, psychiatry and anesthesiology. Our Burn Center research team is nationally and internationally recognized, particularly in the development of artificial skin. Team members contribute to hundreds of medical publications, making significant contributions in virtually every aspect of burn care. Learn more about our Burn Care services below.

Fanny Meisler Trauma Center

The Fanny Meisler Trauma Center at USA Health is the only Level I trauma center (prevention through recovery with advanced surgical care) in southwest Alabama, indicating the highest level of care a facility can provide. We believe that the highest level of care is the only level of care anyone would expect to receive in a life-threatening situation. No matter what day a patient arrives, we have a board-certified trauma surgeon in-house 24 hours a day. Every trauma patient who is brought in is assigned a designated trauma team composed of a trauma surgeon, emergency physician, surgical residents, trauma nurse coordinator, emergency department nurses, radiologic technologist, respiratory therapists, and an anesthesiologist or nurse anesthetist.
